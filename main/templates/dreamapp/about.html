Bridging Code & Consciousness

Analyst · {{ age }} · United States

A unique background bridging technology and psychology. Bachelor of Fine Arts in Industrial Design, full-stack developer in Python, JavaScript, and Solidity — and for the past eight years, a dedicated student of Carl Jung's analytical psychology.

01 · Design

Robert's journey began in the world of design, earning a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Industrial Design with a specialization in Automotive Design.

02 · Code

Drawn to technology, he became a full-stack software developer proficient in Python, JavaScript, Solidity, and various other technologies — building mobile apps, web applications, and Web3/blockchain projects.

"While coding fulfills the technical curiosity, it was a deeper fascination with the human mind that truly shaped the path."

03 · Psychology Awakens

As an INTP — naturally curious and analytical — an unexpected elective university course introduced him to Kant, Descartes, Nietzsche, and Freud. Carl Jung's work, however, was what truly resonated. Jung's theories on the unconscious, archetypes, and personality types gave language to things he had always sensed but couldn't articulate.

04 · Eight Years In

Over the past eight years, Robert has immersed himself in Jungian psychology — exploring the Ego, Shadow, Anima/Animus, and the Collective Unconscious. Dream analysis became a cornerstone of his personal development.

05 · The Personal Reason

His dedication is deeply personal. At age 13, Robert lost his father to suicide — a traumatic event that spurred existential crises and a determination to understand mental health. Jung's work provided not only clarity but also a pathway toward healing and personal growth.

"Through this process of self-discovery, the path became clear: help others on their own journeys."

06 · Today

Robert uses his knowledge of Jungian psychology to help individuals around the world explore their inner selves. Through dream analysis and one-on-one counseling, he guides people to uncover the deeper meanings in their lives — providing insights that lead to growth, healing, and greater self-awareness.
